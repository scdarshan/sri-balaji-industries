About Us

Our journey in the tamarind seed processing industry since 2002

Sri Balaji Industries was founded in 2002 by SK Krishnamurthy with a vision to excel in the tamarind seed processing industry. Under the current leadership of SK Chandra Shekar, our company has grown to become a trusted name in producing high-quality tamarind seed kernel, tamarind seed husk, and tamarind seed pieces.

With over two decades of experience, we have perfected our processing techniques to ensure optimal quality and consistency in all our products. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for businesses across various industries.
